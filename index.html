Account Deletion

Learnify Scan & Study Tools by Softexa.tech

This page explains how users can delete their account and associated data.

How to Delete Your Account

  • Open the app
  • Go to Settings
  • Tap on Delete Account
  • Confirm the action

Your account will be permanently deleted from our system.

What Data is Deleted

  • Your authentication data (Google account connection)
  • Any account-related information stored in our system

What Data May Remain

  • Locally stored notes or scanned files on your device
  • This data can be removed manually by uninstalling the app or clearing app data

Data Retention

We do not retain your personal account data after deletion. Some non-personal analytics data may be retained for a limited period for service improvement.
